Welcome to the Akan Name Generator

Originating from the Ghanaian culture, the Akan names are a set of names given to their children that corresponds to the particular day of the week they were born.

These names, make up many of Ghanaians first names and are what they call “day names”.

This well thought out naming system is continuing to attract many people\’s attention, Ghanaians and others alike.
